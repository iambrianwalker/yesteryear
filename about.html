About Us

Yesteryear ATL is an independent, Black-owned music platform rooted in the heart of Atlanta. Founded in 2023, we specialize in curating, promoting, and amplifying the voices of the city’s underground hip-hop scene. From raw street anthems to experimental sounds, our mission is to highlight emerging artists and give them a space to be heard—authentically and unapologetically.

What began as a passion project quickly grew into a trusted source for discovering Atlanta’s next wave of talent. Through thoughtfully curated playlists, artist showcases, exclusive releases, and consistent digital presence, we bridge the gap between underground culture and the mainstream conversation.

This isn’t about trends or algorithms.
It’s about the city, the craft, and the people shaping what’s next.

Whether you're an artist looking to be heard or a listener searching for something real, Yesteryear ATL is where the future of Atlanta hip-hop lives.
